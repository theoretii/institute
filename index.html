Theoretical Intelligence Institute (TII)

The Institute of Theoretical Intelligence is a non-profit initiative aimed at conducting scientific research in the field of artificial intelligence. We operate in an informal manner, focusing on the theoretical aspects of AI.

Our mission is to understand the theoretical foundations of artificial intelligence and explore its potential. As a non-profit initiative, we focus on theoretical research that can serve as a foundation for future innovations in the field of AI.

Our works are original creations developed in collaboration with the ChatGPT language model by OpenAI, which enables an in-depth analysis of the issues and the formulation of new hypotheses and approaches in scientific research. ChatGPT supports idea generation, thought structuring, and the introduction of innovative solutions in the modeling of biological systems. Thanks to this collaboration, the works gain an interdisciplinary character, combining biology, automata theory, and modern technologies in the field of artificial intelligence.
